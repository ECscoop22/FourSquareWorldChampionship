Rules of Four Square

The Four Square World Championships is an annual tournament held in Bridgton, Maine, to crown the world champions of the sport. This rulebook serves as the official guide governing gameplay, court specifications, and tournament structure.

  • Ball: 8.5-inch rubber playground ball, 2 lbs pressure
  • Court: Four 8x8 ft squares numbered 1–4 (16x16 ft total)
  • Serve: From square 4 to 1, non-aggressive and below head height
  • Outs: Includes illegal hits, double bounces, out-of-bounds, interference, etc.
  • Special: “Out-of-Play Zone” for Championship Round — 10 ft boundary discourages unreturnable hits
  • Divisions: Boys 14 & under, Girls 14 & under, Men 15+, Women 15+
  • Tournament: 3 rounds, with top 8 per division reaching the final “10-out” Championship Round
