🌍 About the Championship

Since 2005, Bridgton, ME, has hosted the Four Square World Championships.

Entrance to the Championship is open to the public so expect people of all shapes, sizes and stature in attendance. Competitors will battle for the greatest four square title in the world. View the rules.

📚 Championship History

From 2005–2022, Squarefour.org covered all things related to the World Championship. For past champions and archived coverage, visit Squarefour.org.

Since 2023, this site has taken over as the home for not only the World Championship but also as the central one-stop shop for all things Four Square.
